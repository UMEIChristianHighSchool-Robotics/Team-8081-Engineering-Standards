# Leadership Roles

This document defines leadership structure and responsibilities.

Clear roles:
- Prevent confusion
- Improve accountability
- Reduce burnout
- Increase student ownership

---

# 🎓 Student Leadership Structure

## Team Captain

Responsibilities:
- Overall coordination
- Inter-team communication
- Culture and accountability
- Match strategy communication

---

## Mechanical Lead

Responsibilities:
- Oversees CAD standards
- Approves fabrication
- Coordinates build schedule
- Ensures serviceability

Owns:
- Drivetrain
- Mechanism reliability
- Structural integrity

---

## Electrical Lead

Responsibilities:
- Maintains wiring standards
- Owns CAN ID map
- Manages battery health
- Oversees electrical inspections

Owns:
- Power system reliability
- CAN bus health
- Sensor integration

---

## Software Lead

Responsibilities:
- Enforces Git workflow
- Maintains architecture standards
- Reviews pull requests
- Oversees autonomous strategy

Owns:
- Robot stability
- Code documentation
- Release tagging

---

## Strategy Lead (Optional)

Responsibilities:
- Game analysis
- Match scouting
- Alliance coordination
- Autonomous prioritization

---

# 🧑‍🏫 Mentor Roles

Mentors:
- Guide, not control
- Approve major decisions
- Ensure safety
- Provide technical depth

Students lead execution.

---

# 🔁 Cross-Functional Expectations

Leads must:

- Meet weekly during build season
- Communicate subsystem changes
- Avoid siloed decision-making
- Document major decisions

---

# 🛑 Leadership Principles

- Leaders enable others.
- Leaders document decisions.
- Leaders remain calm under stress.
- Leaders prepare backups.
- Leaders prioritize safety.

Leadership is service, not authority.
